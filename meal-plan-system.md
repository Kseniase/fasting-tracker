- **100g+ fat:** Primary fuel source, keeps you satisfied, supports hormones

## The Weekly System

### Saturday: Plan & Shop

1. **Review last week**
   - What worked?
   - What didn't get eaten?
   - Any meals that felt too hard?

2. **Select this week's proteins** (pick 3-4)
   - Slow cooker protein (pork or beef)
   - Sheet pan protein (chicken thighs)
   - Fresh-cook protein (salmon, steak)
   - Eggs (always)

3. **Select vegetables** (variety of colors)
   - 2-3 for roasting
   - Raw veggies for snacking
   - Leafy greens for salads
   - Cooking greens (spinach, bok choy)

4. **Generate shopping list** (from selections)

5. **Shop** (or order delivery for Sunday morning)

### Sunday: Batch Prep (3-4 hours)

**Hour 1: Start slow cooker, prep proteins**
- Season and start pork/beef in slow cooker (4-5 lbs)
- Form beef patties, refrigerate
- Season chicken thighs for sheet pan

**Hour 2: Vegetables**
- Sheet pan 1: Chicken thighs (start first, 45 min)
- Sheet pan 2: Broccoli, brussels sprouts
- Sheet pan 3: Zucchini, peppers
- Stovetop: Sautéed greens

**Hour 3: Raw prep & eggs**
- Hard boil 2 dozen eggs (Instant Pot: 5-5-5 method)
- Cut raw veggies (cucumber, celery, peppers, carrots)
- Wash and store salad greens
- Wash berries, portion into containers

**Hour 4: Sauces, assembly, storage**
- Make dressings/sauces (chimichurri, ranch, vinaigrette)
- Shred/portion slow cooker meat
- Container and label everything
- Portion snacks into grab-and-go containers

### Monday-Saturday: Execute

**Daily rhythm (2 meals + snacks):**

| Time | Meal | Target |
|------|------|--------|
| 11am-12pm | Meal 1 (Big Salad/Bowl) | ~600-700 cal, ~50g protein |
| 6-7pm | Meal 2 (Protein + Sides) | ~800-900 cal, ~80g protein |
| As needed | Snacks | ~200-300 cal, ~20g protein |

**Assembly time:** 5-10 minutes per meal (everything prepped)

### Saturday Morning: Weekly Check-in

1. **Weigh-in** (same time, after bathroom, before eating)
2. **Log how you felt** (energy, hunger, mood)
3. **Note what worked / didn't**
4. **Plan next week** (repeat cycle)

## Protein Components

Prep ~10-12 lbs raw per week → ~7-8 lbs cooked

| Component | Prep Method | Raw Amount | Cooked Yield | Protein/serving | Cal/serving |
|-----------|-------------|------------|--------------|-----------------|-------------|
| Pulled pork shoulder | Slow cooker 8-10 hrs | 4-5 lbs | ~3 lbs | 25g per cup | 200 |
| Pulled beef chuck | Slow cooker 8-10 hrs | 4-5 lbs | ~3 lbs | 28g per cup | 220 |
| Chicken thighs (bone-in, skin-on) | Sheet pan 400°F 45 min | 3-4 lbs | 8-10 thighs | 28g per thigh | 180 |
| Beef patties (80/20) | Grill or cast iron | 2 lbs | 8 patties (4 oz each) | 25g per patty | 250 |
| Hard boiled eggs | Instant Pot 5-5-5 | 2 dozen | 24 eggs | 6g per egg | 70 |
| Salmon fillet | Fresh, grill or bake | 1-2 lbs | 4-6 portions | 25g per 4 oz | 200 |
| Bacon | Oven 400°F 20 min | 2 lbs | ~1.5 lbs | 10g per 3 strips | 150 |
| Breakfast sausage | Pan fry | 1 lb | 8-10 links | 8g per 2 links | 180 |

### Protein Prep Notes

**Slow Cooker Pulled Pork:**
- 4-5 lb pork shoulder
- Season: salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, cumin
- Add ½ cup water or broth
- Low 8-10 hours or High 5-6 hours
- Shred, store in cooking liquid

**Slow Cooker Pulled Beef:**
- 4-5 lb beef chuck roast
- Season: salt, pepper, garlic, onion powder
- Add 1 cup beef broth
- Low 8-10 hours
- Shred, store in liquid

**Sheet Pan Chicken Thighs:**
- Pat dry, season liberally with salt, pepper, garlic powder, herbs
- Skin side up on sheet pan
- 400°F for 40-45 minutes until skin crispy
- Internal temp 175°F

**Hard Boiled Eggs (Instant Pot):**
- 1 cup water, eggs on trivet
- High pressure 5 minutes
- Natural release 5 minutes
- Ice bath 5 minutes
- Peel, store in fridge up to 1 week

### Sauces & Dressings (Make Weekly)

**Simple Vinaigrette:**
- ¾ cup olive oil + ¼ cup vinegar (red wine, balsamic, or apple cider)
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard, salt, pepper
- Shake in jar, keeps 2 weeks

**Chimichurri:**
- 1 cup parsley, ¼ cup olive oil, 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 3 cloves garlic, ½ tsp red pepper flakes, salt
- Pulse in food processor, keeps 1 week

**Ranch (quick version):**
- ½ cup mayo + ½ cup sour cream
- 1 tsp each: garlic powder, onion powder, dill, chives
- Splash of lemon juice, salt, pepper
- Keeps 1 week

**Guacamole:**
- 2 avocados, mashed
- Lime juice, salt, cilantro, jalapeño (optional)
- Make 2x per week (browns after 2-3 days)

## Adjustment Guidelines

### If weight loss stalls (>2 weeks no change)

1. **Check compliance first** - are you actually hitting targets?
2. **Reduce calories by 100-200** (drop fat slightly, keep protein)
3. **Add movement** - walking, light activity
4. **Check sleep and stress** - cortisol affects fat loss

### If losing too fast (>1 kg/week consistently)

1. **Add 100-200 calories** (more fat or protein)
2. Muscle loss risk increases with faster loss

### If hungry all the time

1. **Check protein** - hitting 150g?
2. **Add more vegetables** - volume and fiber
3. **Check fat intake** - too low?
4. **Check sleep** - poor sleep increases hunger hormones

### If energy is low

1. **Check calories** - too aggressive?
2. **Check carbs** - might need slightly more (up to 100g)
3. **Check electrolytes** - still need them post-fast
4. **Check sleep** - prioritize 7+ hours
