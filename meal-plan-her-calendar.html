Daily Supplements (~60g)

Breakfast (7am)

3 eggs scrambled + 250g veggies = 21g

2nd Breakfast (10am)

150g Greek yogurt + 50g berries = 15g

Afternoon (3pm)

200g cottage cheese = 24g

Skip when meal has eggs or high protein (steak day)

Reheat Meals

Beef Stew (300g)

Microwave 2-3 min until hot

Chicken Plate

175g chicken + 100g mash + 100g broccoli

Microwave 2-3 min. Add butter to mash.

Pork Power Bowl

125g pork + 100g zuc + 100g brus + 1 egg

Pulled Pork Plate

125g pork + 75g slaw + 100g peppers

Cook Fresh

Salmon (Thursday)

  • Season 125g salmon, salt/pepper
  • Sear skin-down 4 min, flip 3 min
  • Warm 100g broccoli, add 1 egg

Steak (Saturday)

  • Room temp 150g steak 15 min
  • Season, sear 3-4 min/side
  • Rest 5 min, top butter

Bacon Chicken (Sunday)

  • Wrap 175g chicken in 25g bacon
  • Bake 400°F 35-40 min
