# Weekly Meal Plan - Her

**Daily Target:** 120g protein | 1400-1500 calories
**Portion Rule:** 1x (base portions) + daily protein supplements

## The System

**Meals provide ~60g protein** (two 1x portions)
**Supplements provide ~60g protein** (eggs, yogurt, cottage cheese)
