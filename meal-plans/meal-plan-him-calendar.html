Reheat Meals

Beef Stew (600g)

Microwave 3-4 min until hot

Chicken Plate

350g chicken + 200g mash + 200g broccoli

Microwave 3-4 min. Add butter to mash.

Pork Power Bowl

250g pork + 200g zuc + 200g brus + 2 eggs

Microwave 3 min. Add hot sauce.

Pulled Pork Plate

250g pork + 150g slaw + 200g peppers + 50g bacon

Assemble Fresh

Cobb Salad

150g greens + 350g chicken + 50g bacon + 2 eggs + 100g avocado

Warm chicken if desired. Layer, add dressing.

Mediterranean Salad

150g greens + 350g chicken + med mix + 100g avocado

Warm chicken if desired. Drizzle dressing.

Breakfast Salad

150g greens + 50g bacon + 4 eggs + 100g avocado

Warm bacon in pan, toss greens in fat. Top with eggs, avocado.

Cook Fresh

Salmon (Thursday)

  • Season 250g salmon, salt/pepper
  • Sear skin-down 4 min, flip 3 min
  • Warm 200g broccoli, add 2 eggs

Steak (Saturday)

  • Room temp 300g steak 15 min
  • Season, sear 4 min/side
  • Rest 5 min, top butter
  • Sauté 100g spinach in drippings

Bacon Chicken (Sunday)

  • Wrap 350g chicken in 50g bacon
  • Bake 400°F 35-40 min
  • Warm 200g broccoli
