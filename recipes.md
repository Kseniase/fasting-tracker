## Meal 1 Recipes (Big Salads & Bowls)

Target: ~650 cal, ~55g protein, ~13g carbs, ~55g fat

### The Classic Cobb
**Protein: 58g | Carbs: 10g | Fat: 52g | Calories: 680**

- 3 cups mixed greens
- 1.5 cups pulled pork or chicken (shredded)
- 2 hard boiled eggs, quartered
- 3 strips bacon, crumbled
- ½ avocado, sliced
- ¼ cup cherry tomatoes, halved

**Assembly:** Arrange greens, top with rows of each ingredient. Drizzle dressing.

### Classic Steak Dinner
**Protein: 75g | Carbs: 14g | Fat: 55g | Calories: 840**

- 10 oz ribeye or NY strip
- 1 cup roasted broccoli
- 1 cup sautéed spinach with garlic
- 2 tbsp butter (for steak and veggies)
- Salt, pepper, garlic

**Prep:**
1. Bring steak to room temp, season generously
2. Sear in cast iron 4 min per side (medium-rare)
3. Rest 5 min, top with butter
4. Serve with reheated roasted broccoli and fresh sautéed spinach

## Sunday Prep Recipes

### Slow Cooker Pulled Pork
**Yield: ~3 lbs cooked (12 cups)**

**Ingredients:**
- 4-5 lb pork shoulder (bone-in or boneless)
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- 1 tsp cumin
- ½ cup water or chicken broth

**Instructions:**
1. Pat pork dry, rub with all spices
2. Place in slow cooker fat-side up
3. Add water/broth
4. Cook LOW 8-10 hours or HIGH 5-6 hours
5. Internal temp should reach 195-205°F
6. Remove, shred with two forks
7. Return to cooking liquid, mix well
8. Store in containers with some liquid

**Storage:** Fridge 5-7 days, freezer 3 months

---

### Slow Cooker Pulled Beef
**Yield: ~3 lbs cooked (12 cups)**

**Ingredients:**
- 4-5 lb beef chuck roast
- 2 tsp salt

- 1 cup beef broth

**Instructions:**
1. Season beef on all sides
2. Place in slow cooker
3. Add broth

### Sheet Pan Chicken Thighs
**Yield: 8-10 thighs**

**Ingredients:**
- 3-4 lbs chicken thighs, bone-in skin-on
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 1 tsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp paprika
- Fresh thyme or rosemary (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F
2. Pat thighs very dry with paper towels (key for crispy skin)
3. Toss with olive oil and all seasonings
4. Arrange skin-side up on sheet pan, not touching
5. Roast 40-45 minutes until skin is golden and crispy
6. Internal temp 175°F
7. Let rest 5 minutes before storing

**Storage:** Fridge 4-5 days. Reheat in 400°F oven to re-crisp.

### Instant Pot Hard Boiled Eggs
**Yield: 24 eggs**

**Ingredients:**
- 24 eggs
- 1 cup water
- Ice bath

**Instructions:**
1. Add water to Instant Pot
2. Place eggs on trivet (can stack)
3. Seal, set to HIGH PRESSURE 5 minutes
4. Natural release 5 minutes
5. Quick release remaining pressure
6. Transfer immediately to ice bath 5 minutes
7. Peel (shells slide right off with this method)
8. Store unpeeled or peeled in fridge

**Storage:** Unpeeled 1 week, peeled 5 days

### Sheet Pan Roasted Vegetables
**Yield: 8+ cups**

**Broccoli & Brussels:**
- 2 heads broccoli, cut into florets
- 2 lbs brussels sprouts, halved
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder

**Zucchini & Peppers:**
- 4-6 zucchini, ½" rounds
- 4-6 bell peppers, strips
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, pepper, Italian herbs

**Instructions:**
1. Preheat oven to 400°F
2. Toss vegetables with oil and seasonings
3. Spread on sheet pans in single layer (don't crowd!)
4. Roast 20-30 minutes, tossing halfway
5. Vegetables should be browned at edges
6. Cool, store in containers

**Storage:** Fridge 5-6 days

### Cauliflower Mash
**Yield: 4 cups**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 heads cauliflower
- 4 tbsp butter
- ¼ cup heavy cream
- Salt, pepper, garlic
- Fresh chives (optional)

**Instructions:**
1. Cut cauliflower into florets
2. Steam or boil until very tender (12-15 min)
3. Drain VERY well (squeeze out water)
4. Add to food processor with butter, cream, seasonings
5. Process until smooth
6. Taste, adjust salt

**Storage:** Fridge 5 days. Reheat with extra butter.

---

### Simple Vinaigrette
**Yield: 1 cup**

**Ingredients:**
- ¾ cup olive oil
- ¼ cup red wine vinegar (or balsamic, apple cider)
- 1 tsp Dijon mustard
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- Salt, pepper

**Instructions:**
1. Add all ingredients to jar
2. Shake vigorously
3. Taste, adjust acid/salt

**Storage:** 2 weeks at room temp, shake before using

---

### Chimichurri
**Yield: 1 cup**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 cup fresh parsley, packed
- ¼ cup fresh oregano (or 2 tsp dried)
- 4 cloves garlic
- ½ cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- ½ tsp red pepper flakes
- Salt to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Pulse herbs and garlic in food processor
2. Add oil, vinegar, pepper flakes, salt
3. Pulse to combine (leave slightly chunky)

**Storage:** Fridge 1 week

---

### Quick Guacamole
**Yield: 1.5 cups**

**Ingredients:**
- 2 ripe avocados
- Juice of 1 lime
- ¼ cup cilantro, chopped
- 2 tbsp red onion, minced
- 1 jalapeño, seeded and minced (optional)
- Salt to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Mash avocados to desired chunkiness
2. Mix in lime juice immediately (prevents browning)
3. Add remaining ingredients
4. Taste, adjust salt and lime

**Storage:** 2-3 days max. Press plastic wrap directly on surface.

## Quick Reference: Build Your Own

### Salad Formula
1. **Base:** 3 cups greens
2. **Protein:** 50-60g worth (see components)
3. **Vegetables:** 1 cup raw or roasted
4. **Fat:** ½ avocado OR 2 tbsp dressing + cheese/nuts
5. **Bonus:** Hard boiled eggs, bacon, olives

### Dinner Plate Formula
1. **Protein:** 8-10 oz meat/fish (70-80g protein)
2. **Vegetable 1:** 1 cup roasted
3. **Vegetable 2:** 1 cup greens or different roasted
4. **Fat:** 1-2 tbsp butter or oil
5. **Bonus:** Eggs, bacon, cheese

### Snack Formula
Pick 2-3 per day totaling ~200-300 cal:
- **Protein-forward:** Eggs, jerky, deli meat, cottage cheese
- **Fat-forward:** Cheese, nuts, guacamole, olives
- **Produce:** Raw veggies, berries (with protein/fat)
