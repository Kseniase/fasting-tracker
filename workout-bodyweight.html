Morning Start Ritual

  1. Tall glass of water (+ pinch salt)
  2. 2-3 min light movement
  3. Shake out, roll neck/shoulders
  4. Begin workout

No coffee until after workout. Water first, move second, caffeine third.

Warm-Up (Strength Days)

  • 20 arm circles each direction
  • 10 leg swings each leg
  • 10 slow bodyweight squats
  • 5 push-up to down dog

Rest: 60-90 sec between rounds

Progressions

Push-upsArcher, decline
SquatsPistol, pause
Glute bridgeSingle leg, elevated
PlankLonger, add movement
LungesJump, deficit

Goal: Consistency over intensity. Show up every day, adjust as needed.
