Quick Reference KETO ORDERING

Magic Words

"No bun" / "Protein style" / "In a bowl" / "Lettuce wrap" - these work at most restaurants

Always Skip

Fries, chips, bread, rice, beans, tortillas, breaded items, sugary sauces

Upgrade Strategy

Double the meat for more protein without extra carbs. Add cheese/bacon for fat.

Breakfast = Easy

Eggs, sausage, and bacon are naturally keto. Skip the hash browns and toast.
